A Pledge To My Child

Keagan Gilmore
Like a fire I watch you grow You grow, and change everything... You leave no choice but a new beginning But how be fire when you can do no wrong?
How dare I sentence you to me! As you cling to my unworthy finger, I weep.
You gave me the world's richest joy, I robbed you of your righteous right. You cannot know the fears I have As I mumble a sorrowful lullaby.
The dark day dawns dauntingly As I oath to give you an opportunity May you always be in comfort as you rest And along your path to success be blessed
What secrets do you hold For I will dedicate my life to nurturing them I kiss those puffy ocean eyes As I forbid my poverty from infecting you
Like a fire, I will grow I will burn all that tries to harm you I pave the path, for you to fulfill your potential But as a fire, I will raise a saint
